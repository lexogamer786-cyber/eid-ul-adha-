🐐   Qurbani ka Bakra   🐐

Qurbani — The Sacred Sacrifice

Eid ul Adha mein hum Hazrat Ibrahim (AS) ki yaad mein
qurbani karte hain — ek sunnat jo hume yad dilati hai
ke Allah ki raza ke liye sab kuch karna chahiye.

"Teri qurbani ka gosht ya khoon Allah tak nahi pahuncha,
lekin teri taqwa zaroor pahunchi."
— Surah Al-Hajj 22:37

Eid ki Duaein

🕌

Imaan

May your faith grow stronger with every Eid that passes.

❤️

Mohabbat

May love and harmony fill your home and your heart.

💰

Barkat

May Allah shower endless blessings and rizq upon you.

🌿

Sehat

May you and your family be blessed with health and happiness.

🤲

Qubuliyat

May Allah accept your prayers, your Qurbani, and your deeds.

⭐

Khushi

May every moment of this Eid be filled with pure joy.

Eid ki Dua

تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ

"Taqabbal Allaahu Minnaa wa Minkum"
May Allah accept (good deeds) from us and from you.
